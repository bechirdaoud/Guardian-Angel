Model outline

Suggested sections to build a proper investor-grade projection.

1) Acquisition

  • Purchase price, taxes/fees, broker fees
  • Survey + immediate rectification budget
  • Initial safety kit + spares + upgrades

2) Annual operating costs

  • Crew payroll + rotation + travel
  • Insurance (hull, P&I), compliance
  • Maintenance & consumables
  • Berthing / marina / yard
  • Fuel, water, communications

3) Capex & refit cycles

  • Rigging cycles, sails, engine major service
  • Electronics refresh, batteries, generator
  • Paint, teak, interior

4) Governance & owner programme

  • Usage calendar: weeks per owner, guest rules
  • Decision-making: capex thresholds, voting
  • Exit / buy-sell clauses, valuation mechanism
