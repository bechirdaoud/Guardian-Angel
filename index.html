Vessel overview

Guardian Angel is built around the Oyster 885 concept: a powerful cruising yacht with a proven offshore pedigree. The focus is simple — effortless long passages, a calm motion at sea, and the kind of onboard living that makes time away feel like home.

  • Long-range autonomy (fuel + water capacity suited to remote cruising)
  • Stability and seakeeping designed for serious ocean miles
  • Owner-friendly layout with crew-support options
  • Systems designed for maintainability and uptime
