Ownership

Shared programme (5 owners)

A simple structure: clear capitalisation, clear annual budget, and transparent usage rules. Adjust the numbers below to fit your final plan.

Usage principles
Clear calendar, fair allocation

Example: 5 shares (20% each). Each share receives a minimum of 4 prime weeks per year, plus additional weeks subject to availability.

Onboard costs

While an owner is onboard, trip-specific provisioning (food, guest services, special requests) can be charged directly to that owner to keep the shared annual budget predictable.

Booking idea

“Tambola” style draw: a random order is generated for owners to pick first blocks. The previous winner moves last next round to keep the system fair.
